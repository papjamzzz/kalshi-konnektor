MISSION BRIEF

Kalshi Konnektor scans every open prediction market and scores them 0–100 for mispricing probability. High score = the market hasn't figured out the right price yet. That's your edge.

5-STEP DEPLOYMENT
01
Launch the app
Double-click KalshiEdge on your Desktop. The dashboard comes up instantly — data loads in ~20 seconds in the background.
02
Read the table
Markets sort by score, highest first. Score 70+ = strong signal. Click any row to open the full breakdown.
03
Dial the FX knobs
Hit FX in the header to open the signal chain. Twist the knobs to weight what matters most to you right now.
04
Open on Kalshi
Click a market row → hit Open on Kalshi to jump straight to the order book and trade.
05
Star your watchlist
Star markets you're tracking. Toggle Watchlist only to focus. Data refreshes every 60 seconds automatically.

THE SIGNAL CHAIN

Every market runs through 4 independent signal pedals. Each pedal asks a different question about mispricing. The final score is their combined output.

⏱
TIME DECAY
Pedal 1 — Time remaining

Markets far from expiration have more time for the crowd to be wrong. The longer the runway, the higher this score. Normalized over 30 days.

High score = lots of time left for you to be right
📊
SPREAD DRIFT
Pedal 2 — Bid/ask spread

A wide bid/ask spread means market makers aren't confident either. Nobody has converged on the "right" price. Wide = uncertain = edge opportunity.

High score = market makers are uncertain too
🎯
BASELINE DEVIATION
Pedal 3 — Distance from 50/50

Markets near 50¢ are in genuine uncertainty territory — the crowd is split. Markets near 0¢ or 100¢ mean high conviction. Near 50 = maximum mispricing potential.

High score = genuinely uncertain outcome
🏷
CATEGORY EFFICIENCY
Pedal 4 — Market type multiplier

Some categories attract more sophisticated traders (Politics, Economics) and price efficiently. Others (Entertainment, Companies) get less attention and misprice more often.

SCORE INTERPRETATION

The score (0–100) represents estimated mispricing probability. It combines all 4 signal pedals, weighted by volume pro-rating.

70–100 2–3% POSITION

Strong mispricing signal. All 4 pedals are aligned. The market is thin, uncertain, and hasn't converged. Act with conviction.

60–69 1–2% POSITION

Solid signal. At least 3 pedals are firing. Worth a measured position. Watch for movers.

50–59 0.5–1% POSITION

Weak-to-moderate signal. Something's there but it's noisy. Small bet or watchlist only.

<50 WATCH

Market is fairly priced, already liquid, or closing soon. Not worth capital today. Keep an eye in case conditions change.

VOLUME WEIGHTING

Thin markets (under 500 contracts) get a 2× score boost — the crowd hasn't found them yet. Deep markets (over 100k) get a 0.25× dampen — the crowd has already corrected them. This is your thin-market alpha advantage.

CONTROL REFERENCE

Every knob, button, and filter — what it does and when to use it.

🎛 FX PEDAL BOARD
SIGNAL knobWeights the 3 signal pedals (time, spread, baseline) relative to each other. Right = more weight to time decay. Left = more weight to spread.
DEPTH knobControls volume dampening. Right = thin markets get a bigger boost. Left = flattens the volume weight — treats all volumes equally.
TIME knobApplies a gain multiplier to the final score. Right = everything gets amplified. Left = everything gets compressed. Like a master volume.
BYPASSDisables FX and returns to raw base scores. Use to compare your dialed-in view against the unweighted signal.
🔍 FILTER BAR
Category pillsClick to filter by market category. Click again to deselect. Multiple selections not supported — pick the category you're focused on.
Score slidersThe two number boxes are min/max score thresholds. Type a number to narrow the table to only markets in that score range.
SearchLive search by market title or ticker. Runs on the local data — no extra API calls.
Watchlist onlyToggle to show only starred markets. Stars are saved in your browser — they persist across refreshes.
